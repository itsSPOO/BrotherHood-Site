FiveM Server Features (GTA V)

Total Features

Approximately 192 carefully categorized features covering the full gameplay experience on the server.

Main Groups

The server is organized into main groups to streamline player experience and maintain balanced gameplay: Core, Standalone, Dev, QBCore, Casino, Voice, AntiCheat.

Core Stack

The server includes a set of essential tools and systems that ensure stable performance and smooth gameplay: Framework, Advanced Database, Shared Utilities, Connection Queue, Modern 3D Voice UI, AntiCheat Protection.

Core Stack Details

Framework, advanced database, shared utilities, connection queue, modern 3D voice UI, and AntiCheat protection.

Core / Framework

Seamless player and job management, complete economy system, in-game commands and events, admin menus, dashboards, stats tracking, and interactive player menus for quick access to tasks and information.

Jobs

Police / EMS / Jail, Mechanic & Towing, Civilian Activities (farming, recycling, daily jobs), and Car Races with organized competitive events.

Economy & Trade

A full and balanced economy: Banks & Shops, Fuel & Crypto systems, and Pawn Shops for buying/selling rare items and equipment.

Housing & Interiors

A complete housing experience: purchase and interact with houses and apartments, diverse interior designs, and systems to personalize spaces based on preferences.

Crimes & Heists

Crime and heist systems: drug trade and black-market interactions, house/store/private robberies, plus weapons and equipment for planning and execution.

World & Utilities

Doors, garages, and city offices management, respawn system and weather synchronization, car keys and vehicle sales, plus small utilities for daily convenience.

Voice & Media

A complete communication and entertainment system: in-game music, advanced radios, TV and CarPlay, plus a modern 3D voice UI for immersive interaction.

Casino

Games such as Blackjack, Roulette, and Slots, plus memberships and casino chips, with a fully detailed casino interior design (MLO).

Dev / Extra Resources

Additional tools and resources: vehicles, audio and handling configs, extra RP systems (heists, crafting, teleport), HUD/chat/emotes/admin tools, maps/buildings, QoL tools, police add-ons, clothing customization, experimental systems, and large systems like phone and money laundering.

FiveM Jobs (GTA V)

The server provides a wide variety of jobs for a fully immersive RP experience, with daily tasks and specialized roles for each player to make each job feel realistic and exciting.

Police / EMS / Jail

Law enforcement patrols, EMS rescues and emergency response, and prison systems with daily activities like incident investigation, inspections, and large-scale event participation.

Mechanic & Towing

Vehicle repairs and upgrades, towing and recovery, daily service calls and requests, fully equipped workshops, customer-based customization, and direct interaction with the in-game economy.

Car Races

Organized racing events with prizes, competitive driving challenges, daily participation and time tracking, fair result systems, constant player interaction, and car customization/upgrades for every event.
